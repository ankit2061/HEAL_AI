Document Guidelines

Required Documents

To process your claim efficiently, please upload the following documents:

  • Medical bills or invoices
  • Doctor's reports or prescriptions
  • Insurance policy details
  • Claim forms (if already completed)

Document Quality

For optimal processing speed, ensure your documents are clear, legible, and complete. Make sure all important details like dates, names, and amounts are visible.

Privacy & Security

All your documents are securely encrypted and processed in compliance with healthcare privacy regulations. Your sensitive information is protected at all times.
